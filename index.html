Frieren: Beyond Journey's End

Sōsō no Furīren

Frieren

An elven mage who lived for over a thousand years. Calm, distant, and contemplative, she begins to understand the value of time and human emotion long after her hero party's journey ends. Her story is one of self-discovery and learning to cherish fleeting moments.

Himmel

The brave and charismatic hero of the original party. Though long deceased at the story start, Himmel compassion and optimism deeply influence Frieren and shape the journey she takes afterward

Fern

A calm, intelligent, and emotionally mature mage who serves as Frieren apprentice. Raised by the priest Heiter after losing her family, Fern brings a grounded, practical perspective to the journey. Despite her young age, she highly skilled in magic and often acts as the emotional balance to Frieren aloofness. Quietly caring and observant, she shows strength in subtle ways.

Stark

A courageous but soft-spoken warrior who was trained by Eisen, the dwarf of the original hero party. Although he often anxious and self-deprecating, Stark is fiercely loyal and shows immense bravery when it matters most. With a kind heart and a tendency to get flustered, he adds humor and warmth to the group dynamic, especially in his budding bond with Fern.
